Tourist Location


These are some worthy place to visit for a tourist and a college student such as me. The first image is Dufan and the second one is Ancol. Dufan is more of a Playground Rides type of place because there are so many rides to ride there and even though it was built a long time ago, the hype still holds up until now. Ancol is a place near the beachside of Jakarta, so if you're looking for a tan in sunny weather you could go to Ancol and the place is also near Dufan. The third place is PIK "Pantai Indah Kapuk" or Kapuk Beach is also a place near the beachside of Jakarta and the place is also really beautiful.
