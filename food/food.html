Foods


There are some Traditional Food in Jakarta such as Kerak Telor on the left side of the pic, Gado-gado on the middle, and Soto Betawi on the right side. Kerak Telor is an egg but there are some spices added to make it more tasty while gado-gado is mostly a vegetable with sometimes a sweet/spicy-ish sauce and it is very tasty. Soto Betawi is also delicious because of its spices on the soup and it also has some vegetable and some meat in it.
